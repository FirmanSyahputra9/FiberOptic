Fiber Optics Quiz

1. What is fiber optics?




Correct answer: b) Thin glass or plastic fibers used to transmit data using light

2. The main components of fiber optic cables are:




Correct answer: b) Core and cladding

3. Who is NOT known as the "father of fiber optics"?




Correct answer: c) Samuel Bonardy Hutagalung

4. The first successful application of fiber optic technology appeared with the invention of the gastroscope in:




Correct answer: c) 1956

5. Today, fiber optic cables are primarily used for:




Correct answer: b) Transmitting various forms of long-distance communication

6. One of the advantages of fiber optics compared to copper is:




Correct answer: b) Higher bandwidth

7. Which of the following is NOT a main disadvantage of fiber optics compared to copper cables?




Correct answer: a) Can be found everywhere

8. What is a characteristic of multimode fiber optics?




Correct answer: b) Has a core diameter five to ten times larger than single-mode fiber

9. What is a characteristic of single-mode fiber optics?




Correct answer: b) Has a thin core, and light signals travel straight through the fiber without bouncing off the sides

10. What is the transmission distance difference between multimode and single-mode?




Correct answer: b) In single-mode, signals can transmit over longer distances than in multimode
